About Me

This is the head priest (Peethadhishwar) of the sacred place Rudrani Mahakalika Shaktipeeth. Walking on the path of spirituality since childhood and receiving the company of various saints and sages, he was honored with the title of Peethadhishwar Mahant in 2002 and was given the seat (Gadi). For the past 24 years, Maharaj has been worshipping Mahakalika ji during Navratri while observing complete fast, silence, walking barefoot, and performing prayers at 4:00 AM in the Brahma Muhurta.

Famous for several successful predictions in astrology, Maharaj continuously preaches about collecting one roti (bread) from every household for Gau Mata (Mother Cow), that parents are the world's greatest heroes, Kanya Bachao Kanya Padhao (Save the Girl Child, Educate the Girl Child), and water conservation.

Being proficient in Yoga Vidya (science of yoga), Maharaj provides yoga education to various schools, police departments, and colleges. He works daily for the education of girls. Famous for his accurate predictions, distinguished people meet Maharaj daily to receive guidance and blessings.
